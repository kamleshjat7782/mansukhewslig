Eligibility Criteria

To apply for Mansukh Group EWS / LIG housing scheme, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • The applicant must be a citizen of India and a resident of Rajasthan.
  • The applicant must be at least 18 years of age at the time of application.
  • For EWS: Annual household income must not exceed ₹3 Lakh per annum.
  • For LIG: Annual household income must be between ₹3 Lakh and ₹6 Lakh per annum.
  • The applicant or any family member must not own any pucca house anywhere in India.
  • Applicants must belong to one of the listed categories as defined in the scheme.
Note: Submitting an application does not guarantee allotment. Final allotment is subject to verification of eligibility and availability of plots.

Application Process

  2. Review all information carefully before submission — errors may result in disqualification.
  3. Accept the Terms and Conditions and submit the application.
