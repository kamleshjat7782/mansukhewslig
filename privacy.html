Privacy Policy Effective: January 2025

Privacy Policy

Mansukh Group ("we", "our", "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and apply for our housing scheme.

Table of Contents
  1. Information We Collect
  2. How We Use Your Information
  3. Information Sharing & Disclosure
  4. Data Security
  5. Data Retention
  6. Your Rights
  7. Changes to This Policy
01

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you complete the online application form, contact us, or visit our website:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, email address, postal address, pin code
  • Government ID Details: Aadhar number, PAN/Voter ID/Driving License/Ration Card number
  • Financial Information: Annual income range, income category
  • Family Details: Father's name / Husband's name
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited
02

How We Use Your Information

  • Processing your EWS / LIG housing scheme application
  • Verifying your eligibility for the scheme
  • Communicating with you regarding your application status
  • Processing registration fee payments
  • Complying with RERA and government housing norms
  • Sending important notifications about the scheme
  • Preventing fraudulent applications
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.
03

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Government Authorities: As required by RERA, housing authorities, or law enforcement.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors for payment processing and email delivery, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law or court order.
04

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information including SSL encryption, secure data storage, access controls, and regular security assessments.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized use of your information.

05

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as required by law and RERA regulations (typically 5–7 years), or as necessary to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

06

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
