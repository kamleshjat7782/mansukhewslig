Mansukh
Group Story

Mansukh Group is the flagship project of Mansukh Group. Spread across 100 acres, it is perhaps the biggest gated township of Rajasthan.

Much more than just a township, it is the realization of our vision to bring the calm environs of the village close to the city. With luxurious farmhouses, villas and wide open spaces.

We are proud to present the comforts of city life nestled in extensive green expanse. We have already planted more than 10,000 trees and this is just the beginning. It is a stone's throw away from the city centre yet in a cocoon of its own.
